What is Satoshi Pay?

Satoshi Pay is a Bitcoin Lightning and ecash wallet using the Cashu protocol. Send and receive sats instantly with cash-like privacy.

Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA) - no app stores, no gatekeepers. Install directly from your browser on any device.

No KYC

No accounts, no email, no verification. Just a 12-word seed phrase. Your keys, your coins.

Multi-Mint

Connect to multiple Cashu mints. Spread risk, maintain privacy, no single point of failure.

Open Source

MIT licensed. Fork it, customize it, self-host it. Fully transparent and auditable.

Features

Multi-Mint Support

Connect to multiple Cashu mints simultaneously. No single point of failure.

P2PK Token Locking

Lock tokens to specific recipients using NUT-11. Perfect for refunds and recurring payments.

Lightning Interface

Mint ecash from Lightning. Melt ecash to Lightning. Instant swaps between networks.

Nostr Integration

Send tokens via encrypted Nostr DMs. Share your Lightning Address with the world.

Deterministic Restore

Recover your ecash from any compatible wallet using NUT-09 and your 12-word seed.

Offline P2P

Send tokens via QR codes without internet connection. True peer-to-peer digital cash.
